Our Quality Standards

We maintain high standards to ensure all healthcare professionals on our platform are qualified, experienced, and committed to providing exceptional care.

For Nurses & Caregivers

  • Active professional license/certification
  • Minimum one year of clinical experience
  • Clean professional record
  • Up-to-date training and certifications
  • Professional liability insurance

For Patients & Families

  • Access to verified professional profiles
  • Transparent pricing and services
  • Secure messaging and booking
  • Ability to review and rate caregivers
  • 24/7 customer support
